Welcome

Dana is a UKCP registered systemic psycho-therapist, offering individuals, couples or families opportunities to explore the challenges they face in their relationships. She has seen clients in private practice, in Relate centres, and in community settings in London and Cambridge since 2001. An American by birth, Dana trained and qualified in the UK in family therapy and has lived in England for over 22 years.

She is Clinical Supervisor for Relate Cambridge and a tutor for the Institute of Family Therapy in London. She formerly was Programme Leader for the Post Graduate Diploma in Systemic Therapy for the Relate Institute (the training arm of national Relate), and holds an MSc. degree in systemic psychotherapy from the University of Bedfordshire. She previously worked with the homeless in the USA and in the UK and is interested in making her services available to a wide cross-section of the communities in which she lives and works.

Testimonials

Occasionally clients offer to write anonymous statements about the therapy Dana has offered them, and those statements will be posted from time to time. There are also some excellent short videos made by the Association of Family Therapy using actual anonymised feedback from family therapy clients. These may give you a sense of what the potential for family therapy holds for your situation.

Thank you for being the person who was willing to stick with me through out this journey. It really means a lot. A lot of the confidence I have is because of the things you pulled out of me. I was no longer locked in with all this unspoken emotion, and ruled by fear and anxiety. I am truly grateful. I thank God for this stage of the process being over and the residue that remains to continue to fall off me.

Venues

Dana offers individual, couple or family therapy at two venues: central Cambridge and Desborough in Northamptonshire.

Cambridge

Warren Close, CB2 1LE

Tuesday/Wednesday evenings, Thursday mornings/afernoons

Travelling towards Cambridge rail station on Station Road, Warren Close is penultimate right turn before reaching the station, almost immediately opposite Tennison Road.

Bicycles and pedestrians can approach the entrance by passing through the bollards on Warren Close, then finding the buzzer at the first door on the right (the number will be given to clients with a confirmed appointment).

There is no street parking available on Warren Close, but some spaces can be found on Station Road or Tennison Road. One parking space can be arranged in advance through Dana (0795 002 0076).

Desborough

Lower Street, NN14 2NP

Thursday evenings, Friday mornings/afternoons & occasional Saturdays

Desborough is located approximately 5 miles from Kettering and Market Harborough, and is a 30 minute drive from both Leicester and Northampton.

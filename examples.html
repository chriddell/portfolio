Examples

May 2014

Well Fed

Visit website

A portfolio site for a small videography & photography collective based in the north of England, this project was submitted as my final year piece at university (alongside a 10,000 word written report - for which I achieved a First) and involved content, design & development and UX testing as well as guiding the client through initial set-up (server set-up/hosting, domain registration etc.).

The project gave me a first taste of liasing with a real-world client and the communication and issues that go with it. It also allowed me to set-up a real development stack & deployment process for the first time, involving a lot of Git, virtual hosts & symlinks (and headaches...).

March 2013

Dana Mills-Powell

Visit website

The first site I ever built.

This was done for a family therapist based in Cambridgeshire, UK, and it's aim was a basic information site whereby potential clients could read more about the service Dana offered.

Although of a very simple nature, the site brings together wonderful illustrations and straight-forward content in a way that is easy to read and navigate.
